Halloween Quiz

Welcome to the Halloween Quiz Game!

Prepare for a Halloween-themed adventure filled with spooky questions.

To play:

  1. Choose between 3 difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  2. Click "Start Quiz" to begin the Halloween quiz.
  3. Select the correct answer from the options provided.
  4. Click on the next question button.
  5. Beware! Spooky sounds are present in the game! You can mute this with the mute button below.

May the spirits of Halloween guide you to victory! Good luck and enjoy the frightful fun!
