Halloween Quiz

Welcome to the Halloween Quiz Game!

Prepare for a Halloween-themed adventure filled with spooky questions.

To play:

  1. Click "Start Quiz" to begin the Halloween quiz.
  2. Choose your difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  3. Select the correct answer from the options provided.
  4. Watch your score creep up with each correct answer and beware of the ghoulish consequences for incorrect ones.
  5. Click "Next Question" to continue or "Finish" when you've conquered the quiz.
